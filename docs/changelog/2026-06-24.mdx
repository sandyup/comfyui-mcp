_June 24, 2026_

[Krea 2](/blog/krea2-comfyui) shipped its open weights on June 23, and the
`krea2-txt2img` pack lands the next day.

## Krea 2 Turbo, packaged

- **`krea2-txt2img`** — Krea 2 Turbo (Krea.ai's 12B Diffusion Transformer,
  distilled) text-to-image. Runs on **native ComfyUI krea2 support**
  (`CLIPLoader type=krea2`, ComfyUI ≥ v0.26.0) with a **Qwen3-VL 4B** text encoder
  and the **Qwen image VAE**. Generates in **~8 steps at cfg 1**.
- Sliced from the KREA2 ULTRA monolith into a standalone single-pipeline pack.
  The Ideogram-style **JSON prompt-builder**, seed-variance, and laplacian-sharpen
  post-proc ship **bypassed** (opt-in); the manual-prompt path is the default.
- The `ConditioningKrea2Rebalance` safety-filter-bypass node is included but
  **bypassed** — Krea 2's safety filter stays intact by default.
- fp8 build for RTX 4000/3000/2000; swap in `krea2_turbo_mxfp8` on RTX 5000.
